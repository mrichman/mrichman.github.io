--- layout: post title: IIS.net Launches categories: [articles] tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- This morning, Microsoft opened to the public a website dedicated to IIS - www.iis.net.
You'll find forums, whitepapers, webcasts and more all in one place. In addition, a great deal of information about IIS 7 has been placed here. This includes, hands on virtual labs of IIS7, walkthroughs, FAQs and more. Much of it has not been available to the general public until now.
